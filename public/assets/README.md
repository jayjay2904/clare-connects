# Asset replacement guide

- `clare-portrait-placeholder.svg`: replace with an approved portrait at 1600 × 2000 px (4:5), ideally WebP.
- `social-share.svg`: replace with an approved 1200 × 630 px social-sharing image.
- `favicon.svg`: review once the final Clare Connects logo is supplied; add 180 × 180 and 512 × 512 app icons.
- Add approved home, business and connector photography only when Clare holds suitable usage rights. Prefer WebP/AVIF, approximately 1600 px on the longest edge.

Never stretch the logo or place it over a low-contrast photograph.
