# Asset replacement guide

- `clare-headshot-studio.jpg`: approved square studio portrait used in the homepage hero.
- `clare-headshot-warm.jpg`: approved warm portrait used in the Meet Clare section.
- `clare-connects-logo.png`: supplied Clare Connects logo used in the header and footer.
- `clare-connects-team.png`: supplied inclusive team artwork used on the Join the Team page.
- `clare-portrait-placeholder.svg`: retained only as a development fallback; it is no longer displayed.
- `social-share.svg`: replace with an approved 1200 × 630 px social-sharing image.
- `favicon.svg`: derive final 180 × 180 and 512 × 512 app icons from the supplied brand artwork when approved.
- Add approved home, business and connector photography only when Clare holds suitable usage rights. Prefer WebP/AVIF, approximately 1600 px on the longest edge.

Never stretch the logo or place it over a low-contrast photograph.
