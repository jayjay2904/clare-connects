# Clare Connects content checklist

The public website is production-ready with the information currently approved. The following details are deliberately configured as optional values in `src/content/siteContent.ts` so they can be added without redesigning the site.

## Add when Clare confirms them

- `phone`: Clare's preferred public telephone number.
- `whatsapp`: a complete WhatsApp link, such as `https://wa.me/44...`, only if Clare wants enquiries there.
- `social.facebook`: Clare's official public Facebook page.
- `tradingName`: the exact legal or trading name that should appear in the footer and terms.
- `legalStatus`: for example sole trader or limited company, using the wording confirmed by Clare or her adviser.
- `complaintsProcedure`: the approved route and wording for complaints.

## Testimonial attribution

The two approved testimonials are shown without invented names. Each testimonial supports optional `firstName`, `surnameInitial`, `business`, and `location` fields. Add only details the reviewer has permission to publish.

## Before a final legal sign-off

- Ask Clare or her legal adviser to confirm the privacy, cookie and website terms wording.
- Confirm the relationship and commission disclosure against current partner requirements.
- Confirm whether any provider-specific regulatory wording must appear.
- Confirm the service areas and whether Clare supports enquiries outside them.
- Verify all external partner links after any programme or provider change.
